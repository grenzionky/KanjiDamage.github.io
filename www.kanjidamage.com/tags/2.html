BETA

The opposite of SOLO – BETA kanji are only found in compound words, (jukugo）. ‘Beta-beta’ in Japanese means “sticky,” and BETABETA kanji hate to be alone – they like to ’stick’ to other kanji.

This is good news for you, because it means you don’t need to study their KUNyomi!
