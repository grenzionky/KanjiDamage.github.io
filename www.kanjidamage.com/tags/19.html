PN

PROPER NOUN – this kanji is often used in names of people or places. Actually, most kanji CAN be used in proper nouns, but certain ones appear so often you should learn them just for that alone! And THOSE guys get the PN tag.

Incidentally, Proper nouns in Japanese usually but not always take the KUN reading.
